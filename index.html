Pattern Map

Every LeetCode problem falls into one of two categories: a Danger Zone where patterns look similar and you must choose carefully, or an Island with unmistakable structural clues.

🥊The Danger Zones

Patterns share structural similarities. You must actively decide between them based on constraints.

Linear Scanners: Arrays & Hashing vs. Two Pointers vs. Sliding Window
Decision Makers: Backtracking vs. 1-D DP vs. 2-D DP vs. Greedy
The Floater: Sorting O(n log n) vs. Heap O(n log k) top-K
🏝️The Islands

Dead giveaways in the prompt. Unmistakable structures or explicit keywords.

Linked List: Handed a ListNode (.next only)
Trees: Handed a TreeNode (DFS/BFS)
Tries: "Autocomplete" or "dictionary prefix"
Stack: Matching brackets, LIFO, "next greater"
Binary Search: Sorted array + O(log n) required
Graphs: "Networks," "cities," "flight paths," "islands"
Intervals: 2D arrays of [start, end] pairs
Math & Bits: Matrix rotations, XOR, binary shifts
